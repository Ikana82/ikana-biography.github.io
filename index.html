Who Was Vincent Van Gogh

Painter

Vincent van Gogh was a painter from the Netherlands. He was born in 1853. He made more than 2,000 artworks. Today, many people love his paintings, but when he was alive, he only sold a few.


At first, he painted dark pictures about farm life. Then he moved to Paris and saw colorful art. He changed his style and used bright colors. In a town called Arles, he painted some of his best works like Sunflowers and The Bedroom.


Van Gogh had mental health problems. He felt very sad and even hurt himself. He went to a hospital but still kept painting. He died in 1890. Now, people see him as one of the most important artists in the world.
